Meet Kelly Patrick-Field

Counsellor & Founder

”GROWING CALM MINDS AND CONFIDENT HEARTS, ONE CHILD AT A TIME”.

“I believe every child is unique with the inner strength to grow, they just need a little support finding the right tools.”

Based in Werribee South, I am a qualified therapeutic counsellor with a passion for supporting neurodiverse children and their families. With experience in child development and emotional wellbeing, I provide a compassionate, strengths-based approach that celebrates each child’s individuality. At Minds at Peace, I offer a safe and nurturing space where children can explore their feelings, build resilience, and develop practical coping skills. My sessions integrate mindfulness, Cognitive Behavioural Therapy (CBT), and psychoeducation, helping children understand their emotions and experiences while fostering confidence and self-awareness. Children are invited to express themselves through talk therapy, creative activities, and even the use of musical instruments, allowing them to connect and communicate in ways that feel natural and meaningful. Every session is tailored to your child’s unique needs and interests—creating space for growth, understanding, and positive change, one mindful step at a time.

KIND THOUGHTS KIND WORDS KIND ACTIONS was a mindful based programme I developed and implemented across three campuses whilst working at a local Specialist School Setting. This programme was so successful it gained recognition from Parliament where our school was presented with an award. The programme taught students how mindfulness enables thoughtful decision-making and empowers them to control their reactions. The data collected during this programme showed a significant improvement in student behaviours , thus profoundly impactful.
